Usage: playlistlist

Permissions:
  i_playlist_needed_view_power

Description:
  List all available and viewable playlists.
  Own playlists are shown, ignoring the view power

Example:
  playlistlist
  playlist_id=10 playlist_title=Yet\sanother\splaylist playlist_type=0 playlist_owner_dbid=1 playlist_owner_name=owned\svia\sbot needed_power_modify=-2 needed_power_permission_modify=-2 needed_power_delete=-2 needed_power_song_add=-2 needed_power_song_move=-2 needed_power_song_remove=-2|...
  error id=0 msg=ok
