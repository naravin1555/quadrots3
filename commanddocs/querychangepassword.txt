Usage: querychangepassword client_login_name={username} [client_login_password={new password}]

Permissions:
  b_client_query_change_password
  b_client_query_change_own_password

Description:
  Change the password for the given query.
  You could generate a new one or change the password to a specific one.

Example:
  querychangepassword client_login_name=server_bot
  client_login_name=server_bot client_login_password=l7ShF3FMOURn
  error id=0 msg=ok
